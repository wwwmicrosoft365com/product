]]> Downloading and Installing Microsoft Office 2013 on Windows or Mac platform is an easy task. Microsoft Office is one perfect solution for the user who wishes to work on documents related to charts, statistics, table, document, visual presentation, and others. From schools to university everyone is using the Office productivity suite, as it has a highly user-friendly interface to work on.

In this blog we will provide you with the instructions to start with Office setup 2013 on your Windows-based computer, follow the instructions and get started.

Note: Before starting to download or to install this software, check if there is any other version of Microsoft Office product is installed on your computer or not. If yes, then uninstall it otherwise it will be the cause Office installation errors and poor performance of the PC.

How to download and Install Microsoft Office setup 2013

Method 1: Using CD/DVD

  1. Redeem Office product key from the retail card you purchased
  2. Insert the CD/DVD in the space and let your computer/ laptop read it
  3. A pop-up will appear, click Office setup 2013 and continue
  • If you do not see any pop-up on the screen,  then open my computer option available in the  windows explorer and choose the E: drive disk option
  1. Select ‘Run Office Setup’
  2. Start the installation
  3. Enter your MS Office product key in its respective field
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts and complete the process

After all the above steps for the completing the task, the software will take some time for the installation process. Make sure no other program running in the background otherwise it will interrupt the running installation process. Otherwise, you will get an error.

Another way to install Office 2013 is via online MS Office website. Perform the succeeding steps.

Method 2: Visiting office.com/setup

  1. Go the mentioned link and when the page appears click Sign in/ Sign up
  2. Complete the login and follow the step when redirected
  3. Enter your Office product key in the field
  • If you have not purchased a subscription, visit the main Microsoft Office setup site and get your subscription
  1. You can choose to work online and if you want to download Office setup 2013 on the computer, you will find the download option on the home page of your account
  2. Click download and complete the installation as directed

Now, the software is successfully installed on your computer. In case you encounter an error, submit your report on office.com/setup.
