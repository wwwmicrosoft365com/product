]]> Microsoft Office setup applications have been produced for the client with various comfort, regardless of whether they like to get to it on their PC or on an iOS gadget. One such application from the Office setup profitability suite is, OneNote that qualifies clients to make notepads and areas on OneDrive from your Android or iOS gadgets. You can perform numerous different assignments too, however for that, you need an active membership of office set up so you can download and introduce the application to work on it.

Other than installing, if you wish to synchronize the Office setup OneNote to your iPad from your PC, ensure you have an active account on www.office.com/setup. In the case if  you don’t have a active account on office.com/setup at that point make your account and enter your Office setup product key in the field to get the account.

When done, follow the below method to introduce Microsoft Office setup OneNote on your iPad.

Steps to install and synchronize Office com setup OneNote on iPad

  1. Tap App Store to launch it
  2. Browse for Microsoft Office OneNote to download and press the search icon to have options
  3. Tap on OneNote icon and proceed to download it
  4. Tap Get it
  5. If a license and agreement page appears, tap on the checkbox to mark and allow the installation
  6. Now, tap on the Office setup OneNote icon to launch it on the screen
  7. Use your account credentials associated with office.com/setup or Microsoft account to login
  8. Follow the prompts and sync your PC’s OneNote data with your iPad or any iOS device

You have successfully synchronized OneNote with your iPad. You can now effectively access and make notes even you are making the most of your excursion or while ion holidays from the working environment. OneNote on iPad gives you the opportunity to make notes whenever, anyplace.
